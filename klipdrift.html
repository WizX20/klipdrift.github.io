ALBUM ON BANDCAMP

Klipdrift are: Cees Smith (vocal chords and braided beard) Wouter "High Rise" Paap (2 sticks and toms) Nico Bot (4 strings without Panic) Marko "Sir Serb" Šalinić (6 strings and extra vocal chords) Remy Suring (6 strings and extra vocal chords).

ABOUT KLIPDRIFT

This wayward mix of hardrock, heavy metal, new & old wave and a hint of punk makes listening to this band a rollercoaster of diversity and style. The tight and solid rifs, supported by subtle melodic licks makes it impossible for the public to not move to the beat! The sort of raw vocals supported by backing vocals makes the experience complete and unforgettable.
